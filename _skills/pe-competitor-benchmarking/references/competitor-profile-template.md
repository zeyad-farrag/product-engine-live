# Competitor Profile Template

Use this template for every competitor profiled in Phase 2. Complete all 9 sections. Evidence and sources are required for every factual claim.

---

## YAML Frontmatter

```yaml
---
type: competitor-profile
name: [Competitor Name]
market: [source market context — e.g., "UK", "Germany", "luxury Nile cruise"]
destinations: [list — e.g., [egypt, jordan, morocco]]
positioning: [one-line positioning summary — e.g., "Premium Egypt specialist for UK independent travelers"]
threat_level: HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW
created: YYYY-MM-DD
updated: YYYY-MM-DD
confidence: HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW
status: active | superseded | archived
initiative: [producing initiative slug or "standalone"]
tags: [searchable tags — e.g., [egypt, uk-market, luxury, direct-operator]]
---
```

## Confidence Levels

Assign overall profile confidence based on evidence quality:

| Level | Criteria |
|---|---|
| **HIGH** | Direct website research + 20+ customer reviews + OTA listings verified + pricing confirmed |
| **MEDIUM** | Website research + some review data + pricing estimated or partially confirmed |
| **LOW** | Limited web presence, minimal reviews, pricing unknown, significant gaps in profile |

## Threat Level Criteria

| Level | Criteria |
|---|---|
| **HIGH** | Same destinations, same audience, similar price point, strong brand, growing presence |
| **MEDIUM** | Overlapping audience or destinations, but differentiated enough to coexist |
| **LOW** | Peripheral overlap only, clearly different positioning, or declining presence |
