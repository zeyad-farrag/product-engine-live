                       bring a strong academic culture to their travel. The
                       Pharaonic civilization's completeness (monumental

                       a civilization in its entirety. The Grand Egyptian Museum

                       German-speaking Egyptologist guides who can engage in

                       well-executed, knowledge-rich journey. "Alles war

                       A sense of discovery within a framework of certainty.
