# Adventure World — Australian Competitor Profile

**Type:** Australian wholesale/retail travel operator specialising in tailor-made and independent (FIT) travel; operates both B2B (wholesale) and B2C (direct) channels  
**Founded:** 40+ years ago  
**Headquarters:** Level 4, 35 Grafton Street, Sydney NSW; also Melbourne presence  
**Phone:** 1300 363 055 / 1300 295 049 / +61 2 9028 0808 (international)  
**Emergency Contact:** +61 (0) 412 425 416  
**Website:** [adventureworld.com/en-au/destinations/middle-east/egypt](https://www.adventureworld.com/en-au/destinations/middle-east/egypt)  
**Industry Accreditation:** CATO (Council of Australian Tour Operators) accredited wholesaler  
**Confidence Level:** MEDIUM — B2B wholesale model limits publicly available B2C data; key data gap: actual B2B pricing/margin structure unknown

## 1. Presence

- **FACT:** [40+ years of expertise in handcrafting bespoke holidays, operating to 90+ countries](https://www.adventureworld.com/en-au).
- **FACT:** Active Egypt product page updated as recently as February 2026.
- **FACT:** CATO (Council of Australian Tour Operators) accredited wholesaler and/or tour operator — industry-recognised standing.
- **FACT:** Australian office hours Monday–Friday 07:00–17:00 AEST; Sydney office (Level 4, 35 Grafton Street).
- **INFERENCE:** Adventure World is one of the few Australian operators offering Egypt as a tailor-made/FIT product rather than fixed group departures — a fundamentally different model that serves a distinct customer segment.

---

## 2. Product

- **FACT:** [Authentic Egypt: 10 days / 9 nights from A$6,555pp](https://www.adventureworld.com/en-au/tours/authentic-egypt): Private activities with English-speaking Egyptologist guides; Pyramids of Giza, Grand Egyptian Museum, Luxor East Bank, Valley of the Kings, Karnak Temple, Abu Simbel, Khan El Khalili bazaar.
- **FACT:** The 10-day Authentic Egypt itinerary is described as "customisable" by Destination Experts; departs Fridays, Sundays, and Wednesdays — flexible scheduling versus fixed group departures.
- **FACT:** Middle East destination coverage includes Egypt, Jordan, and Oman.
- **INFERENCE:** Primarily a tailor-made/FIT operator for Egypt — the antithesis of the fixed-group escorted tour model used by all other Australian competitors.

- **INFERENCE:** Australian retail travel agents (Helloworld, Flight Centre, and independent agents) can access Adventure World's Egypt product through B2B pricing.
- **FACT:** This makes Adventure World the only Australia-based B2B wholesaler for Egypt product identified in the research.

## 9. Weaknesses / Gaps

- **INFERENCE:** FIT/tailor-made model excludes the significant segment of Australian travellers who specifically want the social and safety benefits of a group departure — especially relevant for the 55+ solo and couple traveller market.
- **INFERENCE:** No Egypt specialist depth beyond the standard circuit (Pyramids, Nile cruise, Abu Simbel, Petra) — the Authentic Egypt itinerary follows the same circuit as every group tour competitor.
- **ASSUMPTION:** Limited solo traveller infrastructure — private tours for a solo traveller at A$6,555pp may be cost-prohibitive vs. small-group alternatives at similar price points.
- **INFERENCE:** B2B model means Adventure World's brand awareness among Australian consumers is low; it sells through agents who present the product under their own brand identity.
