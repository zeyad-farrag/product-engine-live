# TripADeal — Australian Competitor Profile

**Type:** Australian online travel deal/flash-sale platform and direct tour operator  
**Headquarters:** Australia  
**Website:** [tripadeal.com.au](https://www.tripadeal.com.au/tours/egypt)  
**Confidence Level:** HIGH — very transparent AUD pricing; key data gap: group size not disclosed

---

## 1. Presence

- **FACT:** Multiple Egypt and Egypt/Jordan/Turkey packages actively selling with 2026–2028 departure dates, including the ["28 Days Egypt, Jordan & Türkiye"](https://www.tripadeal.com.au/deals/5728-egypt-jordan-turkiye) and ["18 Days Ultimate Jordan & Egypt"](https://www.tripadeal.com.au/deals/4865-Ultimate-Jordan-and-Egypt).
- **FACT:** Operates tours directly (labelled "Operated by TripADeal"), not a reseller of third-party operators.
- **FACT:** [Trustpilot: 801 reviews as of March 2026](https://au.trustpilot.com/review/tripadeal.com.au), confirming multi-year market operation.
- **FACT:** Departures available from Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth, and Auckland — with city-specific price surcharges for each.
- **INFERENCE:** Has been operating Egypt tours for several years given review volume on Facebook travel groups dating to 2022–2024.

## 9. Weaknesses / Gaps

- **FACT:** Non-refundable A$149 service fee and non-refundable deposits create high cancellation friction for Australian consumers.
- **FACT:** No specialist Egypt knowledge — treats Egypt identically to Japan, China, Vietnam, and other destinations in its portfolio.
- **FACT:** Accommodation star ratings are self-reported and repeatedly questioned in reviews; the [August 2025 Facebook thread](https://www.facebook.com/groups/travelat60/posts/3120585291434228/) citing "5-star cruise was actually 2-star" is representative.
- **INFERENCE:** No Egyptologist-trained guides or culturally specialist content programming — guides are generalist tour managers.
- **INFERENCE:** Group sizes are not disclosed; likely significantly larger than boutique operators (ASSUMPTION: 20–40+).
- **ASSUMPTION:** Limited pre-departure personalised support — platform model prioritises volume over service depth.

## Memphis Tours Competitive Advantage vs. TripADeal

TripADeal's strength is price and Qantas Points — it wins the budget/deal-seeker segment. But its structural weakness is that it has **no Egyptian specialist knowledge whatsoever** and **no quality control over in-Egypt operations**, which is why accommodation complaints are chronic. Memphis Tours targets a fundamentally different customer profile.
