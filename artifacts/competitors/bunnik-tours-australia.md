## Memphis Tours Competitive Advantage vs. Bunnik Tours

Bunnik's core strength — trustworthy, family-run Australian brand with strong service recovery — is real. But it is built on top of a fundamental structural weakness: **Bunnik relies on third-party Egyptian ground operators**. The 2018 TripAdvisor Egypt feedback (guide overcharging, below-expectation hotels) is direct evidence of this.
