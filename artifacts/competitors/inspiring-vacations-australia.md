## 9. Weaknesses / Gaps

- **FACT:** Inconsistent on-ground accommodation quality versus advertised star ratings — the single most common negative review theme.
- **FACT:** "First Class" overnight sleeper train in Egypt repeatedly flagged as substandard ("dirty, poorly maintained, low hygiene").
- **FACT:** Poor communication and document delivery before departure — multiple reviews cite receiving documents only 1 day before tour start.
- **FACT:** Tour cancellations due to minimum group numbers not being met — with credit-only (not cash refund) resolution, creating strong customer dissatisfaction.
- **INFERENCE:** Acts as an intermediary/reseller for in-destination ground operators — has little direct control over on-ground quality and is dependent on third-party Egyptian operators.
- **INFERENCE:** Massive discount advertising ("40–47% off") devalues the brand's premium claims and attracts price-sensitive buyers who feel deceived when quality gaps emerge.
